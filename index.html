Master's thesis / Case study 01

Research prototype

Foreign Word Detection Research

Research into identifying words that do not belong to the primary language of a given text, supported by CLI and GUI prototypes of the complete detection pipeline.

Challenge
Detect foreign words relative to a text's automatically identified primary language.
Approach
Build a shared pipeline that detects the main language, classifies candidate words, and returns the detected foreign words through CLI and GUI interfaces.
Evaluation
Compare FastText, DeepSeek v4, and Gemma 4 under controlled conditions using labeled synthetic samples created by injecting foreign words into source texts.
